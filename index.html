Hi. Welcome to Torch.

This is a server, hosted by CCI at FSU, designed as a sandbox for student web development and projects.

Learn how to access Torch.

Here's what you can do:

Host your class or personal websites

Your site can be used for classwork, and after your class is over, continue to develop after your class is over.

Quick start

SSH

ssh FSUID@torch.cci.fsu.edu

SFTP

Host: sftp://torch.cci.fsu.edu
Username: Your FSUID
Password: Your FSU password
Port: 22

Expand your class project into a portfolio project

Harness the power of built-in git, MySQL, PHP, Python3, NPM, etc. to impress employers. HTTPS makes your project look official.

Anything else you can think of

Think we're missing that latest Javascript framework? Need some obscure PHP module? Contact us, and we'll work something out.
