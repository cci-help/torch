Accessing torch.cci.fsu.edu

Accessing torch is easy. Everything is automatic, as soon as your initial connection is completed.

Learn how to access Torch.

Here's how to gain access:

Step 1

Enroll in a CCI web development class, or contact the CCI HelpDesk for access.

Right now, you must be in a web development class at CCI to use Torch. Hopefully, we can expand to all students soon!

Step 2

Use SSH or SFTP to create your website.

Connect once, and your site will be live.

Quick start

SSH (command line)

ssh FSUID@torch.cci.fsu.edu

SFTP (program such as FileZilla or CyberDuck)

Host: sftp://torch.cci.fsu.edu
Username: Your FSUID
Password: Your FSU password
Port: 22

View your website

https://torch.cci.fsu.edu/~FSUID

Don't forget the tilde (~)!

Still having trouble? Open a ticket with the CCI HelpDesk

Step 3

Develop

Check your @my.fsu.edu email for your MySQL account information!

Think we're missing that latest JavaScript framework? Need some obscure PHP module? Contact us, and we can get it installed.
