Faculty Information

Torch is designed to make the grading and instruction easy.

Don't worry about handling login information or technical support. The CCI HelpDesk has you covered.

Here's how it works:

Automatic Enrollment

Contact the CCI HelpDesk with a list of your students, and access will be granted quickly.

Students will be able to SSH or SFTP with your FSU credentials, and MySQL information will be emailed to them.

Security

No longer will students share account information. Each student has unique SSH/SFTP and MySQL information.

Torch also provides SSL to all student websites - you can now create actual login pages that are secured against MITM attacks.

Easily view your student's websites at https://torch.cci.fsu.edu/~FSUID, and browse their files in /web/FSUID.

Technical Information

Student logins are through AD, with user being their FSUID, and group 'domain users'.

A script runs in a systemd service, using inotify (infinite loop) to detect new folders in /home/. A MySQL account is created, with permissions on a database titled "FSUID" and any other database starting with FSUID_
