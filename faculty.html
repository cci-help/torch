Faculty Information

Torch is designed to make instruction easy.

Don't worry about handling login information or technical support. The CCI HelpDesk has you covered.

Here's how it works:

Automatic Enrollment

On day one of class, any student enrolled in a CCI course should have access.

Students will be able to SSH or SFTP with their FSU credentials, and MySQL information will be emailed to them automatically.

Please note: your MySQL information will not be emailed to you, if you only have an @fsu.edu address (e.g faculty, staff).

However, your information will still be emailed to a CCI address, which we can then forward to you.

Security

No longer will students share account information. Each student has unique SSH/SFTP and MySQL information.

Torch also provides SSL to all student websites - you can now create real, secure login pages.

Easily view your student's websites at https://torch.cci.fsu.edu/~FSUID, and browse their files in /web/FSUID or their home folder.

Technical Information

Student logins are through AD, with user being their FSUID, and group 'domain users'.

A script runs in a systemd service, using inotify to detect new folders in /home/. A MySQL account is created, with permissions on a database titled "FSUID" and any other database starting with FSUID_
